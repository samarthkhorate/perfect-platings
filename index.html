About Us

Established in 2015 in Bhosari MIDC, Pune, Perfect Platings has been a leading provider of high-quality zinc plating and surface finishing solutions for industries across various sectors. With a strong foundation built on precision, durability, and innovation, we specialize in delivering corrosion-resistant coatings that enhance the lifespan and performance of metal components. At Perfect Platings, we understand the importance of quality and reliability in metal finishing. Our state-of-the-art facility in Bhosari MIDC is equipped with advanced machinery and eco-friendly plating processes that meet industry standards and customer expectations. We cater to a diverse clientele, including automotive, aerospace, engineering, and manufacturing industries, ensuring superior protection against rust and wear. With years of experience and a team of highly skilled professionals, we take pride in offering customized plating solutions tailored to meet specific industrial requirements. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner for businesses seeking long-lasting, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly plating services. At Perfect Platings, we strive for continuous improvement, embracing the latest technologies and industry best practices to deliver flawless finishes. Whether you need zinc plating, passivation, or other protective coatings, we guarantee exceptional results with a focus on durability and aesthetics. Choose Perfect Platings – where precision meets protection, and quality is our priority!
